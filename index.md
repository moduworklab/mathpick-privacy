Privacy Policy for MathPick
Last updated: February 01, 2026
MathPick ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application "MathPick" (the "App") collects, uses, and discloses information.
1. Information We Collect
1.1 Permissions
To provide the core functionality of solving math problems, the App requires the following permissions:
Camera: Used to take photos of math problems and solutions. The images are processed locally or sent to our AI service provider for analysis and are not stored on our servers permanently.
Microphone: Used for the "Voice & Text" chat feature to convert your speech to text.
1.2 Personal Data
We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number.
1.3 Non-Personal Data
We may collect non-personal data for app stability and improvement, including:
Device information (Model, OS version)
Crash logs
App usage data
2. Third-Party Services
We use trusted third-party services to improve our App and generate revenue. These services may collect information used to identify your device.
Google AdMob: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use device identifiers (like Advertising ID) to serve personalized ads.
Google Generative AI (Gemini): We use Google's AI services to process math problems and generate solutions. Data sent to the AI model is anonymous and used solely for generating the response.
3. Children's Privacy
Our App is intended for general audiences and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.
4. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
